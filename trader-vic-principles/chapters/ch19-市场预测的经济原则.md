Key contents:
- **3 Frameworks**: 市场预测两原则 (Two Principles of Market Forecasting), 经济分析六程序 (Six Economic Procedures: 评估→生产→储蓄→投资→创新→交换), 自上而下三层次分析法 (Top-Down Three-Level Analysis)
- **10 Key Concepts**: including 索罗斯式机会, 价值的主观性质, 基源利率, 风险vs不确定性, 生产优先于消费, 凯恩斯谬误
- **3 Mental Models**: 鲁宾逊荒岛模型, 扑克牌局类比, "条约与玫瑰花"原则
- **4 Anti-patterns**: 过度偏重原则一忽略原则二, 以分析家估值替代市场估值, 将经济理论与政府管理等同, 将货币与信用等同于财富
- **6 Key Takeaways** + **5 Connects To** (Ch 10, 20, 18, 9, 21)

The chapter's central tension is Sperandeo's 1991 mistake: correct economic forecast, wrong trading outcome — because he prioritized fundamental analysis (Principle 1) over market psychology (Principle 2). The chapter teaches the Austrian economics (Mises) foundation needed to identify "Sorros opportunities" where the majority operates on false premises.
